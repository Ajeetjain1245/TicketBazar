Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy tickets?

Click on the event you're interested in, then click the "Buy Tickets" button to proceed with payment.

Can I cancel or refund my ticket?

It depends on the event's policy. Check the event page for cancellation terms or contact the organizer.

How do I create an event?

Click on the “Create Event” link in the top navigation bar and fill out the required details to list your event.

Where can I find my purchased tickets?

You can find your tickets by clicking on “Find my tickets” after logging into your account.

I need more help. How can I contact support?

You can reach us through the Contact Sales page for personalized assistance.
